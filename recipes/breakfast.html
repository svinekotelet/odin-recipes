Kitty Breakfast

This tasty, cat-friendly omelette is the purr-fect way for your furry companion to start the day! It makes good use of shredded carrots, which contain healthy beta-carotene. The eggs and cottage cheese add a bit of protein, and the cottage cheese is also low in lactose, making it easier for your kitty to digest.

Ingredients

  • 3 medium eggs
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 3 tablespoons cottage cheese
  • 2 tablespoons of carrots (when grated)

Steps

  1. Bring a small pot of water to a boil. Cut the carrots into 1/4 inch slices and add to boiling water. Cook for 4-5 minutes or until tender. Cut or shred into small pieces when done.
  2. While the carrots are cooking, combine the eggs and water and beat well.
  3. Pour the eggs in a non-stick pan (to avoid using butter or oil). Cook on medium-low heat until done.
  4. Flip over like a pancake and spread the cottage cheese and veggies over half of the cooked surface. Fold like an omelet. Cool and cut to bite-sized pieces to serve.
